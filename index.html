About The Academy

Formed on parade grounds, built for the whole nation.

Moi Forces Academy Comprehensive School was established to serve the children of officers and civilians alike, combining the structure and discipline of a forces institution with a warm, inclusive learning environment.

Today our boarding and day school welcomes learners from Kenya's Luo, Kikuyu, Luhya, Maasai, Kisii, Kalenjin, Kamba and many other communities, alongside international students. We believe a child who understands where they come from stands taller wherever they go.

Our campus balances rigorous academics with cultural festivals, athletics and a co-curricular programme that has produced national champions in music, rugby and athletics.

Admission procedure

Five steps take a family from first enquiry to their child's first day in uniform.

01

Enquiry & Prospectus

Request our prospectus online or at the front office and confirm grade availability.

02

Entrance Assessment

Learners sit a placement test in English, Mathematics and Reasoning.

03

Parent Interview

A short meeting with the Admissions Panel and, for boarders, a house tour.

04

Offer & Fee Payment

Successful applicants receive an offer letter; a deposit secures the place.

05

Orientation Day

New learners and parents attend orientation before the term begins.

Documents required at application

  • Copy of learner's birth certificate
  • Previous school's report forms (last 2 years)
  • Transfer letter from current school (if applicable)
  • Copy of parent/guardian national ID or passport
  • 4 recent passport-size photographs
  • Immunisation / medical record
  • Completed application form
