About Us

Moi Forces Academy Nairobi

Moi Forces Academy Nairobi is a premier educational institution sponsored by the Kenya Defence Forces. We provide quality education that balances academic excellence with character development, discipline, and leadership skills.

Our school is located in the heart of Nairobi and serves the children of KDF personnel and the general public. We offer a comprehensive curriculum from Preprimary to Junior Secondary education (Grade 1-9), preparing students for national and international life experiences.
