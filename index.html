About our school

Moi Forces Academy Comprehensive is a premier national school rooted in the values of the Kenya Defence Forces. We offer holistic education that fosters academic excellence, discipline, and national cohesion.

“We mold warriors of intellect — proud of our heritage, ready for the future.”

Our co-curricular programs celebrate Kenya’s ethnic diversity, with cultural festivals, music, and drama that feature traditional attire from Luos, Kikuyus, Luhya, Maasai, Kisii, and more.

Our history

Founded in 1982 as a school for children of military personnel, Moi Forces Academy Comprehensive has grown into a multicultural institution that admits students from all counties. We have produced top scholars, sports champions, and leaders who serve Kenya with distinction. Our badge remains a symbol of courage, unity, and academic prowess.
